# PART A — 8 FIXNÍCH FOTEK (hero barva)
> Tyto fotky jsou vždy stejné, bez ohledu na počet barev produktu.
> Všechny v `[HERO COLOR]`. Tvoří "product story" — od hero shotu po emocionální důkaz.

---

## IMAGE 1 — HERO SHOT
**Účel:** Thumbnail v kolekcích, první dojem, Google Shopping feed.

```
Three-quarter angle full body shot. Model positioned at 30-degree angle to camera,
facing slightly left. Weight on right hip, creating natural S-curve silhouette.
Arms relaxed at sides, fingers slightly apart — not stiff, not touching swimsuit.
Model fills approximately 70% of vertical frame. Head to just below knees visible.

Swimsuit: [PRODUCT NAME] in [HERO COLOR — e.g. "rich midnight navy"].
Top: [describe specific top construction — e.g. "sweetheart neckline with adjustable
wide straps, removable soft cups visible through fabric"].
Bottom: [describe specific bottom — e.g. "high-waist panel reaching 3 inches above
navel, wide ruched waistband sitting flat against stomach, full hip coverage"].
Fabric sitting smoothly on body with zero bunching, zero rolling at waistband.

This is the ANCHOR image. All subsequent images must match this lighting exactly.
```

---

## IMAGE 2 — FEATURE CALLOUT / ANNOTATED SHOT
⏸️ **NÍZKÁ PRIORITA — přeskočit při prvním kole generace. Vrátit se později.**

**Účel:** Infografický záběr — popisky ukazující klíčové vlastnosti produktu. Buduje důvěru a komunikuje value props na první pohled.

⚠️ **Generovat ČISTOU fotku bez textu → popisky přidat v Canva jako overlay.**

```
Front-facing full body shot. Model standing straight, arms slightly away from body
(not covering any part of the swimsuit). Neutral confident expression, direct eye
contact with camera. Model fills approximately 75% of vertical frame.
Head to mid-shin visible.

Swimsuit: [PRODUCT NAME] in [HERO COLOR].
Every construction detail must be clearly visible and unobstructed:
— Straps (width, adjustability)
— Neckline/bust construction (support band, cups)
— Waistband (high-waist shaping panel)
— Side details (ruching, ties, seams)
— Overall fit and silhouette

Clean, neutral studio-style background (warm beige or light gray).
Even flat lighting from both sides — minimal shadows so all product
details are equally visible. No dramatic side lighting.

This image is the BASE for adding text callout overlays in post-production.
Leave clear space on both sides of the model for callout labels and leader lines.
```

### Post-production (Canva/Figma):
Přidat **4–6 callout popisků** s leader lines ukazujícími na konkrétní části produktu.
Každý callout = **bold headline + krátký popis (1 řádek)**.

Příklady calloutů (přizpůsobit per produkt):
- **WIDE ADJUSTABLE STRAPS** — No digging. No shoulder pain.
- **SECURE BUST SUPPORT** — Wide underbust band for lift + hold.
- **WAIST-SNATCHING BAND** — High-waist shaping waistband.
- **ADJUSTABLE SIDE RUCHING** — Tie to raise or lower coverage.
- **SMOOTH & SCULPT FIT** — Flattering shape, comfy all day.

## IMAGE 3 — LIFESTYLE / ENVIRONMENT
**Účel:** Emocionální hook — "takhle budeš vypadat na dovolené."

```
Full body shot, slightly wider framing than Image 1. Model walking slowly toward
camera at slight angle, one foot ahead of other, natural stride. Arms swinging
gently or one hand lightly touching hair. Genuine relaxed smile, looking slightly
past camera (not dead-center eye contact). Wind catching hair slightly.

Swimsuit: [PRODUCT NAME] in [HERO COLOR — same as Image 1].
Full product visible from neckline to mid-thigh. Fabric moving naturally with
body motion — no stiff frozen look. Waistband staying in place during movement.

Environment more visible in this shot — background fills 40% of frame.
Shot feels candid and natural, as if photographer captured a genuine moment.
```

## IMAGE 4 — DETAIL / FOCUS AREA
**Účel:** Zákaznice chce vidět jak produkt skutečně sedí na těle. Tummy control důkaz.

```
Tightly cropped shot. Frame from just below bust to mid-thigh ONLY.
No face visible. Model standing straight, slight left hip tilt.
Both hands relaxed at sides, not touching swimsuit.

Swimsuit: [PRODUCT NAME] in [HERO COLOR].

CRITICAL FOCUS AREA: [CHOOSE ONE per product, examples:]
— "Wide ruched high-waist panel sitting smoothly across the stomach
   with no rolling or folding, waistband edge invisible under fabric"
— "Tummy control band lying completely flat with no digging,
   natural skin visible above waistband"
— "Side seam construction and hip coverage — fabric smooth over hip bone,
   no cutting in, leg opening sitting flat"

Fabric texture sharp and highly detailed — weave pattern visible.
Skin texture natural and visible. No excessive retouching on stomach area —
show real fit on real body. Warm side light from left creates subtle shadow
defining the waist shaping effect.
```

## IMAGE 6 — SIDE PROFILE / SHAPING
**Účel:** Ukazuje siluetu a tvarování z profilu — shaping efekt.

```
Full side profile shot. Model facing camera-left, standing straight with slight
natural arch in lower back. Arms relaxed at sides or one hand lightly on hip.
Chin slightly lifted.

Full body from head to knees.
Swimsuit: [PRODUCT NAME] in [HERO COLOR].
FOCUS: Silhouette — the shaping effect of the high-waist panel visible in profile.
Show how the waistband creates a smooth line from ribcage to hip.
Natural bust profile, supported but not pushed up unnaturally.

Warm left-side lighting creates definition along the body's edge.
Background soft, model is a clean silhouette against bokeh.
```

## IMAGE 7 — MATERIAL CLOSE-UP
**Účel:** Kvalita materiálu, trust building — "tohle není aliexpress."

```
Extreme close-up. Frame shows approximately 15×15cm area of fabric ON the model's body
(stomach area or hip area). Skin visible at edges of fabric.

FOCUS: Fabric weave texture at macro level. Individual threads visible.
Elastic waistband edge — clean finish, no fraying. Stitching line — even,
consistent spacing. Fabric lying flat against skin without pilling or snagging.

Swimsuit: [PRODUCT NAME] in [HERO COLOR].

Warm directional light from left creating texture shadows in fabric weave.
Shallow depth of field — center of fabric tack sharp, edges gently soft.

This shot proves the product is high quality. Every stitch must look intentional.
```

## IMAGE 8 — BEFORE / AFTER
**Účel:** Emocionální důkaz transformace. Tummy control selling point.

⚠️ **Generovat jako DVĚ SEPARATE fotky → spojit v Canva s čistým dividerem.**
⚠️ **STEJNÁ modelka v obou panelech** (audience = systém určuje).

### BEFORE panel:
```
Clean white or light gray seamless studio backdrop.
Flat even lighting, no directional shadows.

Full body shot from head to mid-thigh. Wearing a GENERIC plain
black one-piece swimsuit (no branding, no logos, no details — basic).
Swimsuit showing realistic fit problems:
— Waistband visibly rolling down at front
— Fabric bunching at hips
— Straps pulling at shoulders
— Slight muffin-top effect at waistband edge
Posture: shoulders slightly forward, stomach not pulled in,
natural relaxed body. Subtle discomfort — NOT exaggerated or mocking.

NO text, NO labels, NO arrows in image.
```

### AFTER panel:
```
SAME studio backdrop, SAME flat even lighting, SAME framing and crop.

Upright confident posture, shoulders back, slight warm smile,
direct eye contact with camera.

Full body shot from head to mid-thigh. IDENTICAL framing to BEFORE.
Wearing [PRODUCT NAME] in [HERO COLOR].
Swimsuit sitting perfectly:
— Waistband completely flat with zero rolling
— No bunching at hips
— Fabric smooth across stomach and thighs
— Straps straight, not pulling
— Body looks supported and shaped, NOT squeezed
Natural skin texture visible. No excessive smoothing.

NO text, NO labels, NO arrows in image.
```

## UGC / AUTHENTIC SHOT (volitelný bonus — 1× per produkt)
**Účel:** Autenticita a trust. Přidat kamkoliv do galerie jako "reálný" záběr.

```
STYLE OVERRIDE — this image intentionally breaks the polished look:

Shot on iPhone 15 Pro. Slightly warm color cast. Natural daylight, not styled.
Minor lens flare acceptable. Composition slightly off-center (intentional).

Casual relaxed expression — mid-laugh or looking away from camera.
Hair messy/natural (wind, water). No professional makeup.
Visible tan lines acceptable.

Location: REAL environment — backyard pool with visible deck furniture,
OR busy public beach with other people slightly blurred in background,
OR hotel balcony with swimsuit visible.

Candid three-quarter shot. Model not perfectly posed — caught mid-moment.
Phone-quality depth of field (deeper than DSLR — more in focus).

Swimsuit: [PRODUCT NAME] in [ANY COLOR].
Product visible and recognizable but this is NOT a fashion photo.
This is "my friend took this pic of me on vacation" energy.

NO studio lighting. NO perfect skin. NO fashion posing.
GOAL: If a customer saw this on Instagram, they'd think it was a real person's post.
```

## Produkční workflow

### Krok 1: Zafixovat proměnné
Před generací vyplnit:
- `[PRODUCT NAME]` → např. "Isola Sculpt High-Waist Bikini Set"
- `[HERO COLOR]` → např. "rich midnight navy"
- `[VARIANT COLORS]` → seznam dalších barev s přesnými popisy

### Krok 2: Generovat Part A (hero barva)
1. **Image 1** (Hero) — ANCHOR. Vše ostatní se řídí tímhle.
2. ~~**Image 2** (Feature Callout)~~ — ⏸️ přeskočit, nízká priorita
3. **Image 3** (Lifestyle)
4. **Image 4** (Detail)
5. **Image 5** (Back View)
6. **Image 6** (Side Profile)
7. **Image 7** (Material Close-up)
8. **Image 8** (Before/After) — studio, dvě fotky → Canva merge

### Krok 3: Generovat Part B (per color variant)
Pro každou další barvu:
1. **Color Variant** — identická póza jako Image 1, jiná barva

### Krok 4: Post-production
- Canva/Figma: Feature Callout overlay (Image 2) — popisky + leader lines
- Canva/Figma: Before/After composite (Image 8) — čistý vertikální divider
- Color check: Ověřit že barva plavek matchuje napříč záběry
- Square crop: Všechny finální na 1:1 pro Shopify galerii
- Volitelně: UGC shot přidat na konec galerie

### Krok 5: Naming & Upload
```
Part A (hero):
```

## Generátor doporučení

| Typ záběru | Nejlepší generátor | Proč |
|-----------|-------------------|------|
| Hero, Lifestyle, Back, Profile | **Flux 1.1 Pro / Ideogram 3** | Nejlepší konzistence postav a póz |
| Feature Callout | **Flux** (čistá fotka) → **Canva/Figma** (overlays) | Potřeba čistého záběru + text post-production |
| Detail, Material | **Flux + inpainting** | Detail crop — generovat full a cropnout |
| Before/After | **Flux** separate → **Canva** merge | Potřeba dvou konzistentních záběrů |
| UGC | **Flux + "iPhone photography" prompt** | Nejautentičtější look |
| Color Variant | **Flux + img2img / reference image** | Zachovat identickou pózu, změnit barvu |
